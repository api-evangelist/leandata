An AI SDR (AI sales development representative) is autonomous software that prospects, personalizes outreach, qualifies leads, and books meetings across email, chat, and voice. With AI SDRs already in 93 percent of GTM teams, this guide covers what they do, where they break at enterprise scale, and how to make them work.
